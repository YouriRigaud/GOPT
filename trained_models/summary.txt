--------------------------------------------------------
KEY FINDINGS
--------------------------------------------------------

1. CoG constraint (alone)
   - Ratio cost: −16.8% (0.752 → 0.625)
   - Imbalance improvement: −47.7% (0.148 → 0.077)
   - Strong result: the agent genuinely learns to balance the bin.
   - lambda=0.1 confirmed as the best value after tuning
     (0.03 and 0.05 had no effect; 0.1 gave the best tradeoff;
      0.15 showed diminishing returns).

2. Fragility constraint (alone)
   - Ratio cost: −30.1% (0.752 → 0.526)
   - Imbalance worsens: 0.148 → 0.226
   - Fragility masking blocks many placements and terminates
     episodes earlier, restricting the agent's action space.
   - Without CoG guidance, the restricted choices leave the
     bin more uneven.
   - Fragility violation rate = 0.000: the hard constraint
     (masking + episode termination) is perfectly enforced.

3. CoG + fragility (combined)
   - Ratio cost: −48.8% (0.752 → 0.385)
   - Imbalance: 0.185 — worse than CoG-only but better than
     fragility-only (0.226), so CoG is still partially working.
   - The collapse breaks down as:
       fragility alone:          −30% ratio
       interaction (adding CoG): −18% more ratio
   - The agent struggles to learn both constraints simultaneously
     from scratch.
